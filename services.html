Our Services

Mice Breeding

Our mice are bred with care to ensure the highest quality for your pets. We maintain a clean and healthy environment to produce strong and healthy mice. Each mouse is $3.

Rats Breeding

Our rats are similarly bred under optimal conditions, ensuring they are robust and healthy for your reptiles. Each rat is $5.

The Breeding Process

At Rodent Rarities, we follow a meticulous breeding process:

  • Selective Pairing: We carefully select breeding pairs based on genetics, health, and temperament to ensure the best quality offspring.
  • Controlled Environment: The breeding environment is temperature-controlled and regularly cleaned to maintain a healthy space.
  • Monitoring and Care: We monitor the health and growth of both mice and rats, providing them with a balanced diet and regular care.
  • Separation and Weaning: Offspring are separated from the parents at the appropriate age to ensure their independence and growth.
